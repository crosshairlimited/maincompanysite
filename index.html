About Us

Founded in early 2017, Crosshair GAMES LIMITED is the fastest growing game development studio in Bangladesh. We focus on Card, Board and Casual mobile games for South Asia. we are committed to the development of the Game industry of Bangladesh. We have 45+ passionate game professionals and are constantly looking for great talents to join our team.
